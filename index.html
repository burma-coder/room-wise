Features

Everything a small-space
dweller needs

Thoughtfully built for apartments, studios, shared houses — anywhere every centimetre counts.

🏠
2D Floor Plan Builder

Draw your room to scale with an intuitive grid. Mark walls, windows, doors, and alcoves precisely.

Real-size Furniture Library

50+ furniture pieces with accurate real-world dimensions. If it doesn't fit on screen, it won't fit in your room.

Smart Layout Tips

Built-in design guidance helps you think about ergonomics, traffic flow, and natural light — in plain English.
