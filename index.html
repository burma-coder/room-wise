Why I built this

A tiny room, a big frustration,
and no good tools.

A while back I was trying to plan my small room — figuring out where the bed should go, whether a sofa would fit, how much space I'd have left to actually move around. Simple questions, but surprisingly hard to answer.

I tried the tools that came up in search. Some were too complicated, others wanted me to sign up and pay before I could even drag a sofa across a floor plan. None of them felt made for someone just trying to sort out a small space.

So I built RoomWise. No signup. No paywall. Just drag your furniture, see if it fits, and figure out your room — the way it should have been from the start.

I hope this takes at least one thing off your plate.

— RoomWise Founder

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

RoomWise is a free room planning tool. We are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data. The short version: we collect very little, we don't sell anything, and your room layouts stay on your device.

What we collect

Anonymous analytics. We use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit RoomWise and which features they use. This data is anonymous — we cannot identify individual users. You can opt out by using a browser with tracking protection or the Google Analytics opt-out extension.

Feedback form. If you choose to submit feedback, your message and email address (if provided) are sent to us via Web3Forms. We use this only to respond to your message. We do not share it with anyone.

What we don't collect

We do not collect your name, your room layouts, your furniture choices, or any account information. Your room design exists only in your browser — it is never sent to our servers.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish unique visitors. We do not use any other cookies. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

Third-party services

RoomWise uses Google Analytics (analytics.google.com) and Web3Forms (web3forms.com) as third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy. Amazon affiliate links may also appear — clicking them takes you to Amazon, where Amazon's privacy policy applies.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please send us a message via the feedback form.
