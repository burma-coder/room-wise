Design Tips

☀️ Face seating toward natural light, not against it.
🚶 Keep walkways at least 60cm wide between furniture.
🛏️ Leave 50cm on each accessible side of the bed.
📐 Place the largest piece first — it anchors everything.
🚪 Never block a door swing arc.
🪞 Mirrors opposite windows visually double the space.
📺 TV viewing: 1.5–2.5× the screen diagonal distance.
🌿 Plants in corners soften hard lines beautifully.
